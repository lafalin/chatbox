Settings

Everything you set here lives in your browser. Nothing leaves except direct calls to your chosen model provider.

Your conversations and Side Quests live in localStorage, scoped to this browser only.
This app has no backend.
  • Your API key is read in your browser and sent only to the provider you pick.
  • Your messages are sent only to that provider, in the same request.
  • No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry.
  • The whole page is one HTML file. View Source shows exactly what runs.
  • Open DevTools → Network. The only outbound calls go to your provider's domain.
Risks you should still think about:
  • Anyone with access to your browser profile can read your chats and key from localStorage. Use a key with the smallest scope you can.
  • Browser extensions you've installed can read this page like any other. Only trust extensions you'd trust with a password.
  • The provider you connect to (Anthropic, OpenAI, etc.) sees your prompts under their own privacy policy.
